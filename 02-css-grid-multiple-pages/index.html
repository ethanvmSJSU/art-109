[About Me]:

My name is Ethan Vilchis Macedo, I am a Chicano artist and graduate of the Digital Media Arts program at San Jose State University. My work consists of sketches and concepts for character designs, both through digital and physical mediums. I specialize in graphic design, but also in character design and conceptualizing. Many of the works that I often do utilize the concepts of Super Heroes and their backgrounds of who they are, such as why they became heroes to begin with. In this case, many of these superheroes tend to have numerous political backgrounds and reasoning to why, and that is something that I want to consider bringing into my work.

[My Work]:

[THE PRODIGIOUS SPIDER-MAN]

This is of my take on the Amazing Spider-Man, calling him the Prodigious Spider-Man. He sports a similar layout to the classic red and blue tights, but giving him some different colors, and symbols. He is still recognizable, but still my own.

[THE BLACK CAT]

This is my take on the Black Cat, simply titled as Black Cat. I decided to do something different with this character, giving her a different background and also a different type of costume. Instead of the leather tights, I gave her a free runner style costume. More comfort and also just overall better breathing room.

[THE WANDERER] | [SPIDER-SONA]

This is my Spider-Sona. He has a similar style to my Spider-Man, but I gave him some clothing rather than keeping the spandex suit motif. Similar red and black layout, but giving him stripes on his mask, alongside with missing the webbing.
